# the first impressions

It is SO amusing to me that technically this task is VERY adjacent to [[socialize for collaborations]]* but unlike whatever THAT ended up being, I feel like this task is something I look forward to. In my video beginning the task, I even said I was /excited/. I mean, in some ways I think I overestimated the excitement, I just was relieved and I chalked it up to feeling excitement.

BUT. I do not mean to say I am feeling bad about this. Not at ALL. 

Recently, having had to especially WRITE a ton about MYSELF (awful, truly awful) I am faced with the truth that I can’t articulate myself well. 

I’ve also realized, that I’ve not really asked for hard constructive feedback on my portfolio site or anything. I think that could be very helpful!

I do realize that I should specifically try to reach out to people for some guidance and mentorship. So let’s do it!
