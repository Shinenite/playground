From May 2023 to June 2024 I enrolled in an online school program at [LABASAD](https://www.labasad.com/en/master/online-master-graphic-digital-design/).

The program consisted of 6 modules:
- Principles of Design
- Editorial Design [(🧡)](https://lainesdesigned.com/work/latvian-gaming-memoirs)
- Packaging Design
- Website Design (ugh)
- Motion Design [(🧡)](https://lainesdesigned.com/work/native-language-type)
- Brand Identity

Overall, I was glad that it gave me an opportunity to work on projects that I wouldn’t have by myself, and it was also really neat to be taught by professionals in the industry! I don’t really see myself enjoying going to a university/college for a creative degree, so this was a great middle ground of the two, for me. 

Maybe one day I will come back to this and share more of my experience, but it might also be something I make a video about. We’ll see!
